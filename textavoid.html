Animation Generalists are versatile artists skilled across various aspects of animation, bringing both creative and technical expertise to projects. From storyboarding and character design to rigging and rendering, their work blends traditional artistry with modern technology to create compelling visual experiences. Originally emerging to meet the growing demand for multifaceted animators, generalists now play a crucial role in both large studios and smaller production teams. By mastering multiple disciplines, they streamline production and bring a unique, cohesive vision to complex projects. The objective of an animation generalist is to deliver high-quality visuals with efficiency and adaptability, enhancing storytelling through a broad set of skills that contribute to the entire animation pipeline.
