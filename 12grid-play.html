TYPE: COMMISSIONED / MEDIUM: 3D PRODUCT ANIMATION
— IN MY EARLY FREELANCING DAYS, I WAS VISUALLY DRAWN TO TECH-RELATED PROJECTS — WORKING WITH CLIENTS IN FIELDS LIKE BIG DATA MINING, TELECOMMUNICATIONS, AND EVEN PROMOTING SAFE WI-FI USAGE, AND INTERESTINGLY, I FOUND THAT MY LACK OF DEEP TECHNICAL KNOWLEDGE ABOUT THE NICHE BECAME AN ADVANTAGE. IT ALLOWED ME TO APPROACH STORYTELLING FROM A REGULAR USER’S PERSPECTIVE, MAKING COMPLEX IDEAS MORE ACCESSIBLE - WHILE MAINTAINING CLARITY. — FOR EXAMPLE, INSTEAD OF USING DENSE TECHNICAL DIAGRAMS, WE CREATED A MINIMAL VISUAL METAPHOR FOR A BIG-DATA MINING APPS BY COMPARING THE DATA FLOW TO A HIGHWAY SYSTEM, WHERE EACH LANE REPRESENTED A DIFFERENT DATA STREAM, SORTING AND DISTRIBUTION. MAKING IT EASIER FOR BOTH TECHNICAL AND NON-TECHNICAL AUDIENCES TO UNDERSTAND. — AS I CONTINUE TO REFINE THIS METHOD, I’M EXCITED ABOUT THE OPPORTUNITY TO ADAPT AND EVOLVE WITH EACH NEW PROJECT, CREATING CONTENT THAT RESONATES AND DEEPENS THE UNDERSTANDING ABOUT THE PRODUCTS.
